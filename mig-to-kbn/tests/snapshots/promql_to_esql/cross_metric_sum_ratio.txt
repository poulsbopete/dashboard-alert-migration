source: sum(kube_pod_container_resource_requests{resource="cpu", cluster="$cluster"}) / sum(machine_cpu_cores{cluster="$cluster"})
feasibility: feasible
warning: Approximated PromQL arithmetic using same-bucket ES|QL math
warning: Dropped variable-driven label filters during migration
warning: PromQL series labels were not retained; output is bucket-level and may collapse multiple source series
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE kube_pod_container_resource_requests IS NOT NULL OR machine_cpu_cores IS NOT NULL
| STATS kube_pod_container_resource_requests_resource_cpu_sum = SUM(CASE((resource == "cpu"), kube_pod_container_resource_requests, NULL)), machine_cpu_cores_cluster_sum = SUM(machine_cpu_cores) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute)
| EVAL computed_value = (kube_pod_container_resource_requests_resource_cpu_sum / machine_cpu_cores_cluster_sum)
| KEEP time_bucket, computed_value
| SORT time_bucket ASC
