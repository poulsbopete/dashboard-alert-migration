source: sum(increase(http_requests_total[5m])) by (instance)
feasibility: feasible
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE http_requests_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS http_requests_total = SUM(INCREASE(http_requests_total, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), service.instance.id
| SORT time_bucket ASC
