feasibility: feasible
warning: Approximated PromQL arithmetic using same-bucket ES|QL math

TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE http_requests_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS http_requests_total_rate_sum = SUM(RATE(http_requests_total, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), service.name
| EVAL computed_value = (http_requests_total_rate_sum / 1000)
| KEEP time_bucket, service.name, computed_value
| SORT time_bucket ASC
