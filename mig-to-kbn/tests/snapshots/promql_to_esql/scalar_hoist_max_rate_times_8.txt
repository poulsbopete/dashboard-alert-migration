source: max(rate(node_network_receive_bytes_total[5m])*8) by (instance)
feasibility: feasible
warning: Approximated PromQL arithmetic using same-bucket ES|QL math
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE node_network_receive_bytes_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS node_network_receive_bytes_total_rate_max = MAX(RATE(node_network_receive_bytes_total, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), service.instance.id
| EVAL computed_value = (node_network_receive_bytes_total_rate_max * 8)
| KEEP time_bucket, service.instance.id, computed_value
| SORT time_bucket ASC
