source: http_requests_total{service="web",status!~".*cam(era)?$"}
feasibility: feasible
warning: Counter referenced without rate(); using LAST_OVER_TIME to preserve raw cumulative value
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE service == "web"
| WHERE NOT (status RLIKE ".*cam(era)?")
| WHERE http_requests_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS http_requests_total = MAX(LAST_OVER_TIME(http_requests_total)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute)
| SORT time_bucket ASC
