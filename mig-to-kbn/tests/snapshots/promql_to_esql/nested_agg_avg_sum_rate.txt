source: avg(sum by (cpu) (rate(node_cpu_seconds_total{mode!~"idle|iowait|steal"}[5m])))
feasibility: feasible
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE NOT (mode RLIKE "idle|iowait|steal")
| WHERE node_cpu_seconds_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS inner_val = SUM(RATE(node_cpu_seconds_total, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), cpu
| STATS node_cpu_seconds_total_avg = AVG(inner_val) BY time_bucket
| SORT time_bucket ASC
