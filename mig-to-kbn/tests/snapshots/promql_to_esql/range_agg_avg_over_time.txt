source: avg(avg_over_time(up{job="prom"}[5m])) by (job)
feasibility: feasible
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE service.name == "prom"
| WHERE up IS NOT NULL
| STATS up = AVG(AVG_OVER_TIME(up, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), service.name
| SORT time_bucket ASC
