source: topk(5, rate(http_requests_total[5m]))
feasibility: feasible
warning: topk() without group labels: collapsed to single-series top N; add preferred_group_labels hint for per-series breakdown
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE http_requests_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS _bucket_value = AVG(RATE(http_requests_total, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute)
| SORT time_bucket ASC
| STATS value = LAST(_bucket_value, time_bucket)
| SORT value DESC
| LIMIT 5
