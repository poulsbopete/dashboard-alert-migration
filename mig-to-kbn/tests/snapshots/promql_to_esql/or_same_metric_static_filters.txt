source: http_requests_total{instance="i",status=~"4.."} or http_requests_total{instance="i",status=~"5.."}
feasibility: feasible
warning: Approximated PromQL arithmetic using same-bucket ES|QL math
warning: Counter referenced without rate(); using LAST_OVER_TIME to preserve raw cumulative value
warning: Rewrote PromQL set-or between same metric as a unified WHERE OR clause
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE service.instance.id == "i"
| WHERE (status RLIKE "4.." OR status RLIKE "5..")
| WHERE http_requests_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS http_requests_total_instance_i_status_4 = MAX(LAST_OVER_TIME(http_requests_total)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), status
| EVAL computed_value = http_requests_total_instance_i_status_4
| KEEP time_bucket, status, computed_value
| SORT time_bucket ASC
