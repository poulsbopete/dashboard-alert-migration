source: sum(rate(http_requests_total{job="web"}[5m])) by (job)
feasibility: feasible
---
TS metrics-*
| WHERE @timestamp >= ?_tstart AND @timestamp <= ?_tend
| WHERE service.name == "web"
| WHERE http_requests_total IS NOT NULL
| STATS http_requests_total = SUM(RATE(http_requests_total, 5m)) BY time_bucket = TBUCKET(5 minute), service.name
| SORT time_bucket ASC
